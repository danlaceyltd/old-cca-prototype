You can make a challenge if you disagree with a change that the VOA has made to your property's valuation. You can't make a challenge on these grounds if the change made by the VOA was aresult of a previous challenge you made.

Youll need to provide:

  • an alternative ratable value (if you disagree with the ratable)
  • the date that the ratable value should start (must be on or after 1st April 2017)
  • rent details for the property (if it is rented)
  • evidence that suports your challenge
  • a statement that explains how your evidence supports your challenge and why your suggested rateable value is correct

If you don't include everything you need, we'll contact you to tell you that your challenge is incomplete. If it's not corrected within the time limit given, your challenge will be invalid.
