[An appointed agent must be able to either do checks, challenges or both on your behalf for this property.]

What is a check?

If any of the factual details about your property or land are incorrect, you (the owner, occupier or authorised agent) need to let the VOA know. Telling us about anything that’s incorrect is known as a check.

If all of your property details are correct but you believe your rateable value is incorrect, you must first complete a check and wait for the outcome before making a challenge.

What is a challenge?

Once you’ve completed a check, the VOA will contact you with the outcome. If you disagree with the outcome of the check or you believe the rateable value of your property is incorrect, you can let us know. Doing this is known as a challenge.

You can’t start a challenge until you’ve received the outcome for your check.
